Temperature and tourism

Chosing your holiday destination includes a lot of variables, from whom the weather expectation is one. Can you see this coherence when visualizing the data?

For countries tourism expenditures are a very important income source. In 2016 the 500 mln. tourists in Europe accounted for 342 dollar total (tourism receipts) (European Union Tourism Trends). However, this important source of income (tourism) depends partly on the unmanageable variable climate. How much does climate, or more specific temperature, influence tourism in a country? Are there more tourists in a warm country? And how is the incoming and outgoing tourism distributed? Do citizens of warm countries stay in their own country more?

Bubble chart

In the bubblechart the relationship between tourism and temperature is shown by size and color of the bubbles. Countries like Italy, France and Spain have relatively big bubbles and relatively warm weather.

Line chart

If you're not only interested in the relationship in a whole year, you can see the development of tourism and temperature over the months in the chosen year in the line chart.

The default is set to Netherlands and 2010.

Pie chart

In the piechart you can see weather residents of a country stay in their own country more (for example because it's a warm country) or go more to foreign countries on holidays.

The default is set to Netherlands and 2010.

Data and variables

Variables

Bubble chart

- Tourism: the number of arrivals at tourist accommodation establishments per country per year

- Temperature: the average temperature of all the weather stations (if data available) in the country per year

Line chart

- Tourism: the number of arrivals at tourist accommodation establishments of the chosen country per month in the chosen year

- Temperature: the average temperature of all the weather stations (if data available) in the chosen country per month in the chosen year

Pie chart

- Incoming: the number of arrivals at tourist accommodation establishments for the chosen country and year, devided by country of residence: reporting country (the chosen country) or foreign country

- Outgoing: the number of trips of residents of the chosen country in the chosen year. The trips are devided in domestic (staying in their own country) or outbound (going out of their country)

Data

  • ECAD

    • - Daily mean temperature TG (Blended ECA dataset)

  • Eurostat

    • - Arrivals at tourist accommodation establishments - monthly data (tour_occ_arm)

    - Number of trips by country / world region of destination (tour_dem_ttw)
